Probability & Statistics — Key Subsets

Probability basics — sample spaces, events, conditional probability, independence, Bayes' theorem.

Distributions — discrete (Bernoulli, Binomial, Poisson) and continuous (Uniform, Normal, Exponential) and when to use each.

Descriptive statistics — measures of central tendency, dispersion, percentiles and visualization (histograms, boxplots).

Inferential statistics — sampling, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing (t-test, chi-square), p-values and interpretation.

Regression & correlation — linear regression, residuals, model fit and basic diagnostics.

Bayesian ideas — prior, likelihood, posterior intuition and simple Bayesian updating.

Applications — uncertainty quantification, predictive modeling, A/B testing, and statistical summaries used in engineering and research.
