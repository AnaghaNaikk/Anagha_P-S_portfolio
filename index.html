What is Probability & Statistics?

Probability and Statistics is a mathematical discipline that deals with uncertainty and data analysis. Probability focuses on predicting the likelihood of events, while statistics deals with collecting, organizing, analyzing, and interpreting data.

It helps convert raw data into meaningful information, enabling informed decisions in uncertain situations.

Real-World Applications

Probability and Statistics are widely used in engineering, computer science, artificial intelligence, medical research, economics, business analytics, quality control, and data science.

Applications include weather forecasting, machine learning models, risk analysis, medical diagnosis, stock market prediction, and performance evaluation.
