Subject Name: Fundamentals of Probability and Statistics

Subject Code: 25EMAB233

Probability deals with measuring uncertainty and predicting the likelihood of events.

Statistics deals with collecting, organizing, analyzing, and interpreting data to make decisions.

What does Probability & Statistics mean?

Probability predicts what may happen in uncertain situations, while statistics explains what the collected data reveals. Together, they form the foundation for data analysis, engineering decisions, and scientific reasoning.
