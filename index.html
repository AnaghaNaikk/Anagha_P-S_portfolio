About the Course

Design and Analysis of Algorithms is the foundation of computer science. It teaches how to develop efficient solutions to complex problems by exploring multiple strategies and evaluating their performance. The course focuses on transforming real-world challenges into logical models and choosing the most optimal approach among many possibilities.

Real world relevance & applications

Shortest path algorithms optimize transportation; greedy and MST algorithms help energy distribution; priority queues and heaps support emergency dispatch. Algorithmic thinking transforms everyday systems into fast, reliable, and intelligent services.
