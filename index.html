Project Overview

This Probability & Statistics project presents the key concepts learned in the course, starting from description of data, basic probability, and random variables, up to statistical inference and hypothesis testing.

The project explains important definitions, formulas, and real-life examples, and includes graphs and tables to support interpretation of data.

Chapter Highlights

1. Description of Data

Types of variables, frequency tables, histograms, ogives, box plots, measures of central tendency and dispersion, and skewness.

2. Probability

Sample space, events, addition and multiplication rules, conditional probability, and Bayes’ theorem with simple applications.

3. Random Variables

Discrete and continuous random variables, expectation, variance, binomial, Poisson and normal distributions.

4. Statistical Inference

Sampling methods, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing for means and proportions, and interpretation of results.
