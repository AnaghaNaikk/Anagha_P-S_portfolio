Subject

Probability deals with measuring uncertainty and predicting the likelihood of events.

Statistics deals with collecting, organizing, analyzing, and interpreting data to make decisions.

Real-World Applicability

Probability and Statistics are applied in engineering design, quality control, data science, machine learning, finance, medical research, and scientific experiments.

What does Probability & Statistics mean?

Probability predicts outcomes in uncertain situations, while statistics explains patterns found in data. Together, they form the foundation of analytical thinking and evidence-based decision making.
